### Mail Server ###

**Features Implemented**
  * SMTP Sender and Listener
  * POP 3 Listener
  * User Account Management

**Future Improvements :**
  * IMAP Protocol
  * Secure Authentication mechanism
  * Handling attachments

**Limitations:**
  * Unable to send or receive mails to other domains due to Spam filter
